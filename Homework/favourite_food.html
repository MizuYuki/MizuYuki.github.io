Mashed Potatoes

Recipe by: Raina Wen

A flavourful dish to satasify your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • Salted Water
  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Seasonings *salt and pepper*

Directions:

  1. Wash potatoes thoroughly with cold water.
  2. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  3. Steam potatoes in salted water for *approx.* 15 mins.
  4. In a small saucepan, heat butter and milk over low heat until butter is melted.
  5. Using a potato masher or electric beater, slowly blend milk mixture into poptatoes intil smooth and creamy.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. The dish is ready to be served.

Optionally, a side of gravy can be added to enhance taste.
